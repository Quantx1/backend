# Quant X — SEBI Compliance-Readiness Audit (2026-07-12)

> Engineering compliance-readiness review, code-grounded (branch feat/xai-redesign). **NOT a legal opinion.** Items marked "needs compliance pro" require a SEBI-registered compliance professional / legal counsel. Produced by a 6-dimension parallel code audit + synthesis.

## Verdict

NOT ready for a paid or public launch — this is an engineering compliance-readiness assessment, NOT a legal opinion. The safety spine is genuinely strong for a pre-launch product: a real out-of-sample / walk-forward promotion gate blocks untested strategies from live money (evaluate_gate to 422 gate_failed), the signal ensemble runs real required models with a hard no-fallbacks/honest-empty contract, per-user broker OAuth uses Fernet encryption with a production key guard, JWTs are signature-verified with RLS on essentially every user table, and disclaimer coverage plus the not-advice/registration-pending posture are broadly consistent. But four classes of defect block deploy and must be fixed first: (1) TWO code paths affirmatively claim Quant X IS a SEBI-registered Research Analyst while every other surface says PENDING and the config default is the sentinel PENDING_APPROVAL — one public/crawlable page and one disclaimer served on every dashboard (verified: proof/models/[slug]/page.tsx:153-154, auto_trader_routes.py:703-705, config.py:92); (2) every REST market-data surface (quotes/OHLC/index/depth/option-chain default DATA_PROVIDER=free=yfinance plus single admin Kite, plus screener.in/jugaad/NSE scrapes) reaches paying and unauthenticated users, exactly the Path-A prohibited pattern, while the correct per-user design exists only on the WS/execution paths; (3) automated real-money order routing carries no algo-ID/empanelment/static-IP/human-in-the-loop and a Zerodha quick-connect scrapes Kite, stores the user's raw password + TOTP seed, and posts to Kite's internal OMS bypassing the licensed API; (4) safety/data-exposure bugs — a Copilot chat IDOR (cross-user read AND write via service-role, verified) and a non-durable kill-switch whose flag mismatch lets the 15:50 rebalance re-open positions the user just killed (verified). Separately, whether the buy/sell/hold verdicts and personalised portfolio actions require RA/IA registration, whether the automated executor needs the 2026 algo framework, and whether admin-Kite redistribution / NSE-screener.in scraping for paying users is permissible are legal determinations the code cannot resolve and MUST be ruled on by a SEBI-registered compliance professional before go-live. Recommendation: land the cheap misrepresentation + data-exposure + safety fixes now; do NOT route real-money automated orders or serve paid NSE data until the compliance-professional review clears.
